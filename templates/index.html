Investment Strategies

Golden Cross

When 20-day SMA crosses above 50-day SMA, it signals strong bullish momentum. Consider allocating 50-70% of your capital.

Buy the Dip

When RSI < 30 and price drops significantly, it's a good buying opportunity. Allocate 20-30% of capital.

DCA

Invest fixed amounts weekly/monthly regardless of price. Best for long-term investors with low risk tolerance.
